About Us

{# #}

Total entertainment solution

Designing, producing and operating any conceivable venue of live entertainment from small exhibits and attractions to live arena shows and full theme parks. With offices in North America, Asia and Europe, we harness some of the most creative and experienced talent in the industry to realize your entire project form conception fo final curtain - or provide a la carte services for any particular aspect of production in between.

What we do

Mirage Entertainment Design is a full-service entertainment design, consultancy and management company with global reach. We have conceptualized, produced, and/or operated some of the largest world-class projects in the entertainment related industry, providing clients with personalized services from planning to deployment on a scalable basis, relative to the needs of your project (or any part thereof). Our difference is what sets up apart. With our many years of broad based experience in conceptualizing, producing and operation, we know how all the pieces fit together to create an ultimate guest experience.

{# #}

Design & Development

We provide every (or any) aspect of project design and development from the “Blue Sky” stage through “Concept”, “Schematic Design”, “Design Development” and into “Construction Design“ along with construction management.

{# #}

Shows & Attractions

Our award-winning projects include some of the biggest and finest live shows in the business, as well as iconic special attractions, such as themed entertainment/shopping mall venues and children’s amusement centers.

{# #}

Consulting Services

We provide professional advice and representation through all phases of your project, from master planning to business development strategies, feasibility studies, full scale operation, merchandising, food and beverage. In addition, we specialize in branding, intellectual properties acquisition and IP implementation. Our depth of experience can carry you to first-class success in the entertainment and leisure industry.

{# #}

Festivals & events

Our projects also include seasonal, high-capacity festivals and event attractions at a variety of famous fairgrounds and special venues on a temporary or travelling basis.

IVAS and The Vibe at Bolong Lake

A comprehensive epicenter of industry, entertainment and creativity

Bolong Lake in Tianjin, China is a massive, monorail-connected, entertainment themed industrial park under a glass atrium. Combining creativity, technology and enterprise in a richly themed atmosphere, it contains a residential area, hi-tech movie/television studios, broadcasting facilities, a world class art school, a performing arts center, visual art galleries, entertainment attractions, retail outlets and unique restaurants to draw visitors and top creative talent to an integrated, all-encompassing commercial/entertainment district. With tenants such as Universal Studios, James Cameron, and the Jim Henson Company already signed up, this revolutionary cultural/industrial hub will make Bolong Lake an iconic landmark destination.

Honcun Aju

Capturing the legendary spirit of the Anhui Women

A beautiful water show extravaganza performed nightly at a fabulous 2,000 seat outdoor theater in the scenic Yellow Mountains of Anhui, China – a destination long favored by regional artists and painters. Based on a traditional legend about the local women, this lyrical production is considered to be one of the most famous attractions at this popular resort area.

The Flood of Mandrill Mountain

A spectacular water stunt show that has been wowing audiences at the OCT East Theme Park in Shensen, China since 2009. In an updated version of this classic pirate tale, freestyle jet-ski and watercraft action combines with high flying, high diving stunt work, explosive fire and water effects, a full-sized shipwreck and a heart stopping motorcycle jump over a collapsing bridge…all leading up to one of the most jaw-dropping climaxes in the business: a giant, roaring flood, as untold tons of rushing water cascade down the mountainside.

Binhai Car Show

Lightning fast action and thundering engines.

The stunt driving show at the Binhai Hangkong Mujian theme park in Tianjin, China, delivers fast action, explosive thrills and pulse-pounding excitement to live audiences on a daily basis. Featuring 5 high performance stunt cars, 2 specialized crash cars, 3 motorcycles, and a quad UTV, all handled by world class stunt drivers to the theme of a cops and crime drama, the Binhai Car Show is the largest production of its kind in Northeast Asia.

STRIKE BACK

Make-believe warfare on a real aircraft carrier

“Strike Back” is a large-scale water stunt show, featuring an actual aircraft carrier (formerly of the Russian Navy) at the Binhai Hangkong Mujian theme park in Tianjin, China. Besides the massive carrier vessel, this spectacular production involves 15 performers, 8 watercraft, 1 motor vehicle and a mini-submarine in an action-packed battle scenario.

Consulting Services

Mirage Entertainment Design provides the professional advice and representation that will smooth your way through the obstacles and challenges associated with any project. Our depth of knowledge and experience in the entertainment field will carry you over the many pitfalls on your way to the realization of your dreams.

Feasibility Studies:

We help you to compile and analyze the data required for the initiation and ultimate success of any live entertainment or leisure project.

Business Planning:

Our expertise in planning and development will help you to achieve the right business model for profitability.

Operational Advice:

Whether you are a new or existing operator, we can provide you with innovative solutions for today’s business challenges. From turn-key projects to focused problem solving, our services are scalable to your needs.

IP Acquisition:

Our Industry connections and know-how can assist you in the creation of original intellectual properties or the acquisition and licensing of established intellectual properties to enhance the popular appeal of your project.

Marketing Strategies:

If you build a better mousetrap, the world will beat a path to your door – but only if they know about it. We can help make this happen with the development of effective marketing and publicity strategies.
